As time goes on after what happened at the playground, plenty of rumors spread throughout the village. Some pretty close to the truth – others blatantly false and even ridiculous. Either way, from what you hear, Richard has apparently left without a trace. Some say he’s plotting against you, and that the rumors have only infuriated him even further. But what really strikes you as surprising, is the looks some of the girls your age are giving you all of the sudden. Clearly, they have gotten an eye for you.
