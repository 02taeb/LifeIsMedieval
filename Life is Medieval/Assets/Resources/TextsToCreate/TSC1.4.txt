“Look, his eyes are opening, Oscar!”
“What a beautiful boy we’ve brought to life, my love.”
You hear the sounds of familiar voices as you open your eyes. You’re struck with a piercing light of all the marvels around you, not really knowing what they are.
“If he looks at my hammer he will grow to be strong!” Oscar says, and points with his finger.
“Don’t encourage foolishness, Oscar! He will gaze at the stars and become a wise little boy!” Your mother replies.
